About the Creator

Hello, I'm Xinyi Zheng!

Welcome to 'Urban Oasis'. I'm a student of the web design course and a passionate indoor plant enthusiast.

Three years ago, I brought home my first Pothos, and I haven't stopped since. My apartment has now turned into a mini greenhouse. The original intention of building this website is that I noticed many beginners feel frustrated when their plants wither. I hope to share the mistakes I've made and the experience I've gained through this simple website, helping everyone easily start their plant care journey.

If you have any plant-related questions, feel free to contact me through the form below!
